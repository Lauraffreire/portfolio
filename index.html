About

Hi, I'm Laura Freire, a designer with a background in Design and Multimedia and a growing interest in interaction design, UX/UI and digital experiences.

Throughout my academic journey, I have explored different areas of design, including visual identity, audiovisual production, motion graphics, programming and interactive projects. These experiences have allowed me to develop a multidisciplinary approach and a strong interest in understanding how people interact with technology.

I enjoy combining creativity, research and problem-solving to create experiences that are intuitive, engaging and meaningful. I am particularly interested in user-centred design and in exploring how digital products can connect people, ideas and technology.
